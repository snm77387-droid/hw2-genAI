1. Business Use Case OverviewThe objective of this project is to develop an AI-powered Customer Service Assistant designed to handle diverse e-commerce reviews. The goal is to improve response efficiency while maintaining a professional brand voice, ensuring customer satisfaction, and—most importantly—mitigating legal and safety risks associated with high-stakes complaints.

2. Model Selection & Technical ImplementationModel: Gemini 2.5 FlashRationale: I selected Gemini 2.5 Flash for its low latency and high instruction-following capabilities. During development, I encountered a deprecation warning for the legacy google.generativeai package and proactively migrated to the modern google.genai SDK (2026 version) to ensure robust HTTP/2 connectivity and future-proof performance.Architecture: The system utilizes a Batch Evaluation Script that processes multiple customer personas (Shipping Delays, Damaged Goods, Legal Threats, etc.) in a single workflow, allowing for rapid testing and prompt refinement.
