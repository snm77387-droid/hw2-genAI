Workflow: Automated Customer Support Response (Negative Review Recovery).

User: E-commerce Customer Service Representative / Small Business Owner.

Input: A frustrated customer review (could include issues like shipping delays, product defects, or poor service).

Output: A polite, empathetic, and professional response that acknowledges the issue and offers a standard resolution (e.g., refund or replacement).

Value: Automating this task ensures consistent brand voice, reduces response time, and prevents emotional burnout for staff dealing with angry customers.
